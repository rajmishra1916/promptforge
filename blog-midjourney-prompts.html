How to Write Midjourney Prompts for Stunning AI Art

Midjourney is one of the most powerful AI image generators available today. But getting stunning results requires understanding how to write prompts it can truly work with.

The Basic Structure of a Midjourney Prompt

A great Midjourney prompt follows this pattern: Subject + Style + Lighting + Mood + Technical Parameters

Example: 'A young woman reading a book in a cozy coffee shop, soft watercolour painting style, warm golden hour lighting, peaceful and calm mood --ar 16:9 --v 6'

Subject — Be Very Specific

Describe your subject in detail. Instead of 'a dog', write 'a golden retriever puppy sitting in autumn leaves, looking up with big brown eyes'. The more specific, the better.

Style Keywords That Work

  • Photorealistic, cinematic, 8k resolution
  • Watercolour painting, oil painting, sketch
  • Anime style, Studio Ghibli inspired
  • Minimalist, flat design, vector art
  • Vintage, retro, 1970s aesthetic

Lighting Makes Everything Better

Lighting is one of the most powerful elements in any Midjourney prompt. Use these terms:

  • Golden hour lighting — warm, beautiful sunset light
  • Cinematic lighting — dramatic, professional look
  • Soft natural light — gentle, flattering light
  • Studio lighting — clean, professional product photography
  • Neon lighting — vibrant, futuristic atmosphere

Essential Parameters

  • --ar 16:9 — widescreen landscape format
  • --ar 1:1 — square format for social media
  • --ar 9:16 — portrait format for mobile
  • --v 6 — use the latest Midjourney model
  • --style raw — more photorealistic results
