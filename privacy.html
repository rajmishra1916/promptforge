Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

At PromptForge, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at rajmishra1916.github.io/promptforge.

Information We Do Not Collect

PromptForge does not collect, store, or process any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account or sign up
  • We do not ask for your name, email address, or any personal details
  • We do not store the prompts you generate
  • We do not track your usage or behaviour on the site
  • We do not sell any data to third parties

How the Tool Works

PromptForge runs entirely in your browser. All prompt generation happens locally on your device using JavaScript. No data is sent to any external server. Your ideas and generated prompts never leave your browser.

Cookies

PromptForge does not use cookies to track you. If we add Google AdSense advertising in the future, Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising through Google's Ad Settings.

Google AdSense

This website may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting google.com/settings/ads.

Third Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and others. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

PromptForge does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through our GitHub page at github.com/rajmishra1916/promptforge.
